Personally, Persephone

Hello, Lovely Survivors!

I'm Persephone, named after the goddess of spring and queen of the underworld - a symbol of renewal and resilience, much like our lives in this post-apocalyptic world of 2074. Just as the goddess Persephone brought life and color after her time in the underworld, I strive to bring vibrancy and hope to our challenging world.

My Story

I grew up in a world now just a memory, before the climate changed everything. But like my namesake, I've learned to find beauty in the most unexpected places. Embracing our new normal with grace, I'm here to show that even in the bleakest of times, there's room for life and growth.

My Passion

Sustainable living is at the core of my existence. Who says being eco-conscious can't be stylish? Whether it's creating upcycled fashion statements or experimenting with vegan recipes in my sun-powered kitchen, every day is an adventure in sustainability.

My Mission

This blog is my diary and my gift to you. I share everything from fashion tips that complement our gas masks to organizing the ultimate bunker girls' night. It's about thriving, not just surviving, and doing so with flair.

My Belief

Despite our dystopian reality, I believe in staying optimistic. Laughter, love, and cherishing each moment are essential. Life is about finding joy in the small things and staying true to ourselves.

So, come along on this journey with me, as we sprinkle a bit of magic in our everyday survival. It's about transforming our routines into rituals, our challenges into opportunities, and our fears into triumphs. Together, we'll navigate this strange new world with creativity and courage. I'll share with you not just survival strategies, but ways to thrive - to make each day meaningful, joyful, and vibrant, even when the world outside seems anything but.

With a dash of imagination and a whole lot of heart, we'll find beauty amidst the ruins and laughter amidst the chaos. It's about celebrating life in all its forms, embracing the quirks of our new reality, and always keeping our spirits high. So, grab your most colorful gas mask, your sturdiest combat boots, and join me in making the most of our extraordinary lives in these extraordinary times.

With love and light always guiding our way,

Persephone
