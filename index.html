Welcome to my World

I'm Persephone, your guide to thriving in post-apocalyptic 2074. Named after the goddess who symbolizes rebirth, I embody resilience in our new reality. Growing up before the climate cataclysm, I've learned to find beauty amidst desolation, much like Persephone's return brings spring. My life revolves around sustainable, stylish living—from upcycled fashion to vegan delights in my solar kitchen. Here, I share tips for living beautifully with gas masks, and how to keep spirits high with bunker parties. Join me in discovering joy and resilience in the smallest moments, lighting our path with optimism and love.

With love and light always guiding our way,

Persephone
